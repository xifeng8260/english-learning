📍 场景：课堂上，老师带着同学们认识沙漠
👩‍🏫
Class, look at this picture. What do you see?
同学们，看这张图片。你们看到了什么？
👦
I see sand and a hot sun. Is it a desert?
我看到沙子和炙热的太阳。这是沙漠吗？
desert 沙漠
👩‍🏫
Yes! The desert is a very hot and dry place.
对！沙漠是一个非常炎热、干燥的地方。
hot 热 · dry 干燥
👧
Is there water in the desert, Ms. Green?
沙漠里有水吗，格林老师？
👩‍🏫
A little! The Sahara has only 5 inches of rain every year. That's very dry!
很少！撒哈拉每年只有5英寸的雨水，非常干燥！
only… every year 句型
👦
Wow, what a dry place it is!
哇，真是个干燥的地方啊！
What a…it is! 感叹句
继续聊 · 最大的沙漠在哪？
👩‍🏫
Tom, do you know where the biggest desert in the world is?
汤姆，你知道世界上最大的沙漠在哪里吗？
biggest 最大的 · world 世界
👦
Hmm… Is it in Asia?
嗯……在亚洲吗？
👩‍🏫
Good try! But no. Africa has the biggest desert in the world.
猜得不错！但不对。非洲有世界上最大的沙漠。
👧
I know! The name of the desert is the Sahara!
我知道！那个沙漠的名字是撒哈拉！
name 名字
👩‍🏫
Excellent, Lily! And about 4 million people live near the Sahara.
太棒了，丽丽！而且约有400万人住在撒哈拉附近。
million 百万
👦
4 million people? That's a lot! But how do they live with so little rain?
400万人？好多啊！雨水这么少他们怎么生活呢？
👩‍🏫
Great question! They find water from rivers and underground. The desert is hard, but people are strong!
问得好！他们从河流和地下取水。沙漠条件艰苦，但人们很坚强！

📍 场景：放学后，Tom 和 Lily 复习今天学的内容
👦
Lily, do you want to visit a desert one day?
丽丽，你有一天想去沙漠吗？
👧
Maybe! But it's so hot and dry. I need lots of water!
也许吧！但太热太干了，我要带很多水！
👦
Right! The Sahara only has 5 inches of rain a year. That's less than my swimming pool!
对！撒哈拉一年才5英寸的雨，比我的游泳池还少！
only… a year 频率表达
👧
Ha! So where is the Sahara again? I forget.
哈！那撒哈拉在哪来着？我忘了。
👦
It's in Africa! Africa has the biggest desert in the world.
在非洲！非洲有世界上最大的沙漠。
👧
Oh yes! And about 4 million people live near it, right?
哦对！而且约400万人住在附近，对吧？
About… people 约数表达
👦
Exactly! I think the desert is amazing. It's so different from our place.
没错！我觉得沙漠很神奇，和我们这里太不一样了。
place 地方
👧
Me too! Our place is wet and green. The desert is hot and dry. They are opposites!
我也是！我们这里潮湿又绿，沙漠又热又干，完全相反！
wet ↔ dry 反义词对比
👦
What a dry place the Sahara is! I want to learn more about it.
撒哈拉真是个干燥的地方！我想了解更多。
What a…! 感叹句复习
👧
Me too! Let's look it up together after school!
我也是！放学后我们一起查资料吧！
对话二亮点：
· only … a year 表达频率（每年只有……）
· wet ↔ dry 反义词自然对比
· What a dry place it is! 感叹句再次出现，加深印象
对话中的词汇 · 点 🔊 听发音
desert  沙漠
"The desert is hot and dry."
hot  热的（反义：cold）
"It's very hot in the desert."
dry  干燥的（反义：wet）
"What a dry place it is!"
place  地方
"Our place is wet and green."
biggest  最大的（big→bigger→biggest）
"Africa has the biggest desert."
million  百万（4 million = 400万）
"About 4 million people live near the Sahara."
