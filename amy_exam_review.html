语音规则复习
📍 试卷原题：根据图片和发音规则选择正确选项
单词发音规则例词
skirt /ɜː/裙子
hair /eə/头发
nurse /ɜː/护士
car /ɑː/汽车
fork /ɔː/叉子
试卷易错点：
"The art teacher drives a car on the farm." — car 不是 fork，注意词形相近但意思完全不同！
"Blair has long fair hair." — hair（头发）vs hare（兔子），发音相同但拼写不同

动物 & be动词搭配
📍 试卷易错：sheep/horses 单复数 + be动词一致性
原题易错分析：
"He has many sheep and tomatoes on the farm." → sheep不变，tomato要变tomatoes
"—Mr Lin, what are those? —They're cows." → those(复数)用are，不用is
"These are horses. They are strong." → these对应复数horses
cow / cows
奶牛 They're black and white.
horse / horses
马 They are strong.
chicken / chickens
鸡 can lay eggs
sheep
羊（单复同形）
易错
🔑 这/那 + be动词 一致性
What are those? They're cows.
那些是什么？是奶牛。（those+are）
These are horses. They are strong.
这些是马，它们很强壮。
Is Miss White in the classroom? Yes, she is.
怀特老师在教室吗？是的，她在。
this/that/these/those 速记口诀：
this（这个）→ is    that（那个）→ is
these（这些）→ are    those（那些）→ are
单数用 is，复数用 are！

🔑 时差句型
In Beijing, it's 7:10 p.m. But in New York, it's 7:10 a.m.
北京晚上7:10，纽约早上7:10。（时差12小时）
In Sydney, it is 9:10 p.m. Time for lunch.
悉尼晚上9:10，是午餐时间。
In London, it's 12:10 p.m. Time for lunch.
伦敦中午12:10，是午餐时间。
解题技巧：时差题要先确定一个城市的时间和事件（吃午餐/起床/睡觉），再用时差推算其他城市处于哪个时段。
天气穿衣对应关系：hot→shorts/T-shirts，cool→jackets，cold→scarves/sweaters
春节安全规则 · 传统文化
📍 [新情境·传统文化] Spring Festival Safety Rules
🕯️
Be careful with fire. 小心用火
If you want to use candles, please ask your parents to help you.
如果要用蜡烛，请让父母帮忙。
🍬
Eat safely. 安全饮食
Before eating, wash the fruit and vegetables. Don't eat too much candy.
吃之前洗水果蔬菜，不要吃太多糖。
🚸
Be safe on the road. 道路安全
Hold your parents' hands when crossing the roads. Don't go at a yellow light or a red light.
过马路握住父母的手，不要在黄灯或红灯时通过。
🙏
Respect traditions. 尊重传统
Don't say bad words. They might bring bad luck. Say "Happy Spring Festival!" or "Guonianhao!" to others.
不说不吉利的话，对别人说"新年快乐"。
🔑 重点词汇
respect
尊重；重视（= follow and value important things）
tradition
传统
alone
独自地（不能独自用蜡烛）
易错点："We can use candles alone." 是 False，原文是要让父母帮忙，不能独自使用！做判断题要看清楚条件状语。
